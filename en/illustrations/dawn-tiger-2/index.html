Dawn #2

Inspired by the Lunar New Year of the Tiger 2022, the DAWN illustrations were created as an art for charity project in close cooperation with the LO-ANH Foundation (LOAN Stiftung). 100% of the profit from all reproductions of this arwork, is donated to the foundation to support disadvantaged children from ethnic minorities in the most remote areas of Vietnam.
