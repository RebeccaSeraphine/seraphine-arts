About Seraphine Arts

Inspired by mother nature, I hope to share the love for our beautiful planet through my art. I am an independent artist based in Germany, specialized on watercolour painting, digital vector illustration & design. Color and nature are at the heart of my work.

My designs are available on RedBubble, Society6 and Displate. I offer commissions for both watercolour painting and digital art - please contact me and I'll be happy to assist with the details.
